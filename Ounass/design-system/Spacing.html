Spacing & Grid

Lumen is built on a 5pt rhythm with three load-bearing values: 15 (viewport margin), 20 (compact rail), and 25 (section padding). iOS canvases are 375pt wide. Corners are soft but never round — 4pt is the house radius.

Scale
Multiples of 4 & 5 used across the system. Stick to these values; avoid arbitrary spacing.
