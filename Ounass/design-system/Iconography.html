05 — Foundations

Iconography

Single-weight line icons on a 24pt grid. 1.5pt stroke, round joins and caps. Always inherit currentColor — no standalone color. Optical sizes at 16 · 20 · 24.

Rules

Icons communicate; they don't decorate. Keep the family tight — every new icon earns its place.

  • Grid 24 × 24 with 2pt safe margin
  • Stroke 1.5pt · round caps, round joins
  • Fill Outline only; never mix with glyph fills
  • Color currentColor — ink on light, white on dark
  • Sizes 16 · 20 · 24 · never scaled unevenly
  • Hit area 44 × 44 regardless of icon size
