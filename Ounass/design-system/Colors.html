02 — Foundations

Colors

A restrained palette — near-black ink, warm neutrals, one bronze accent. Color does very little work in Lumen: it holds attention only on CTAs, state indicators, and the occasional campaign surface. Everything else is ink on paper.

Primary
The voice of the brand. Bronze is the single accent — reserved for primary CTAs and text links. Ink is used for all default text, icons, and secondary buttons.
