01 — Foundations

Typography

A serif/sans pairing. Fraunces carries voice in editorial surfaces — titles, section headers, product names. Nunito Sans handles interface, labels, and body. Use sparingly; never more than two families in one view.

Aa
Display / Editorial

Fraunces

A variable serif with soft terminals. Used for page titles, section headings, and product names. Always paired with tight letter-spacing (-0.01em) and a light weight for luxury feel.

Nunito Sans

Humanist sans for everything UI: navigation, body copy, buttons, forms, product cards, microcopy. Bold, uppercase with 0.5 tracking is reserved for CTAs and eyebrow labels.
