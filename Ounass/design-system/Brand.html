Brand

Lumen is a curated luxury e-commerce brand. Quiet, confident, and editorial — the visual system leans on a light-weight serif wordmark, warm neutrals, and restraint. The brand should feel like flipping through a well-made magazine, not shopping online.

Voice & tone

We write like a trusted friend with taste. Short sentences. No hype. No emoji. Always title case for product names; sentence case for UI.

Do

✓ On brand
  • "The edit begins here."
    Quiet confidence. Editorial cadence.
  • "Ivory Base T-Shirt — 3,850 AED"
    Title case for product. Currency suffix.
  • "Order confirmed. Priority delivery Thursday."
    Matter of fact. Delivers the one thing that matters.

Don't

✗ Off brand
  • "SHOP THE DROP 🔥🔥"
    Shouting. Emoji. Streetwear cadence.
  • "Unbelievable deals you don't want to miss!"
    Hype. Over-promising. Exclamation marks.
  • "ivory base t-shirt - $1050"
    Lowercase. Hyphen. Dollar sign. Sloppy.

Principles

01

Restraint

One voice, one accent, one idea per screen. If something can be removed without loss of meaning, remove it.

02

Editorial rhythm

Treat product pages like spreads. Generous whitespace. Strong hierarchy. Image leads, type follows.

03

Warmth

Never clinical. Bone backgrounds, sand pills, bronze accents keep the interface feeling hand-finished.
